Reading Practice

Luyện đọc hiểu • Đọc đoạn văn ngắn và trả lời các câu hỏi chọn lọc

Lan is a second-year university student. She wants to improve her English because she plans to apply for an international internship next year. Every morning, she spends 20 minutes learning vocabulary. In the evening, she watches short English videos and writes down useful expressions. After three months, she feels much more confident when speaking in class.

1. Why does Lan want to improve her English?

2. What does she do in the evening?

3. After three months, how does she feel?

Kết quả sẽ hiển thị ở đây.
